PeopleHCM - Enterprise HR/HCM Platform

EvonSys | Senior Business Analyst & Functional Lead | Pega PRPC 8.x | 3+ years

PeopleHCM is an enterprise Human Capital Management product built on Pega, serving mid-to-large enterprises for end-to-end HR operations.

Modules Owned

Payroll Processing, Leave Management, Expense Claims, Timesheet Management, ATS/Recruitment, Employee Onboarding, and Employee Self-Service Portal.

Key Contributions

  • Led end-to-end requirement elicitation across all seven modules through workshops, interviews, and JAD sessions with HR, payroll, finance, and IT stakeholders.
  • Authored BRDs, FRDs, user stories, acceptance criteria, and process flow diagrams covering complete module flows.
  • Owned and managed the product backlog; facilitated sprint planning, refinement, sprint reviews, and retrospectives.
  • Defined integration requirements for REST/SOAP APIs with payroll providers, banking systems, and attendance hardware.
  • Designed ESS portal wireframes using Figma and Balsamiq.
  • Improved payroll processing accuracy from 88% to 98% through defect pattern analysis and requirement redesign.
  • Reduced HR admin effort by 60% using workflow automation in leave and expense modules.
  • Cut expense claim processing time by 70% through full digitization of approvals and validations.
  • Achieved 82% post-go-live user adoption via UAT rigor, stakeholder training, and change management.
  • Controlled scope creep by enforcing structured change control and impact analysis across 25+ change requests.

Key Skills Demonstrated

Backlog ownership, stakeholder prioritization, sprint facilitation, roadmap input, story clarity, go-live planning, and change management.

Case Study Hub (Case Study Central) - Marketing Content Platform

EvonSys | Business Analyst | Pega Constellation | Internal Product

Key Features Delivered

  • Case Study Creation Wizard with standardized capture of objectives, problem statements, solution details, quantified results, and testimonials.
  • Content repository with search, filtering, and categorization by industry, customer segment, and solution type.
  • Template library for reusable branded formats across documents and decks.
  • Collaboration tooling including comments, version control, and assignment workflows.
  • Configurable multi-stage approval workflow with SLA reminders and email notifications.
  • Analytics dashboard for lead generation, conversion, retention, and case-study pipeline visibility.
  • CRM integration with Pega and Salesforce linking content to leads, opportunities, and accounts.
  • Role-based access and permission controls for confidentiality and governance.

Key Contributions

  • Conducted discovery and requirements workshops with marketing stakeholders to define scope and pain points.
  • Authored BRDs, user stories, and acceptance criteria spanning all eight feature areas.
  • Defined approval workflow logic including SLA thresholds, escalation rules, and notification triggers.
  • Collaborated with Constellation developers to ensure UX requirements aligned with platform design standards.
  • Defined CRM integration data mapping, sync behavior, and error-handling requirements.
  • Designed wireframes for the creation wizard and analytics dashboard.
  • Coordinated UAT cycles with marketing teams through final business sign-off.
  • Defined and validated access-control matrix for admins, marketers, sales users, and approvers.
