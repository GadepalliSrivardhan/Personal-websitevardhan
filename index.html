  • Reduced HR admin effort by 60% using workflow automation in leave and expense modules.
  • Cut expense claim processing time by 70% through full digitization of approvals and validations.
  • Achieved 82% post-go-live user adoption via UAT rigor, stakeholder training, and change management.
  • Controlled scope creep by enforcing structured change control and impact analysis across 25+ change requests.
